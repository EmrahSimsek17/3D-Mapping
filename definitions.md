# Definitions

## Pose

The position and orientation of a camera in 3D space.

``` math
T=[R|t]
```

## Rotation (R)

Represents camera orientation.

## Translation (t)

Represents camera displacement.

## Ego-motion

Motion of the camera itself.

## Scale

Conversion between relative and metric distance.

## Point Cloud

A collection of 3D points representing scene geometry.

## Map

A geometric representation of the observed environment.

## SLAM

Simultaneous Localization and Mapping.

## Visual Odometry (VO)

Estimate camera motion using only image sequences.

## Visual-Inertial Odometry (VIO)

Estimate motion by fusing camera and IMU measurements.

## Depth Estimation

Estimate scene depth from one or more images.

## 3D Mapping

Reconstruct a consistent 3D representation of the environment.
